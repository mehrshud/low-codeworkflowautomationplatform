# Competitive Analysis
## Market Landscape Overview
The low-code workflow automation market is rapidly growing, with a increasing demand for platforms that can simplify complex workflow automation using Large Language Models (LLMs). The market is dominated by a few key players, including n8n, LangChain, and Agentic AI. These platforms offer a range of features, including visual workflow design, AI-powered automations, and integrations with various tools and APIs.
